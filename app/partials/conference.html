Keynote Speakers

Gary Jacobs 9:00 AM Managing Director / Jacobs Investment Company

Gary Jacobs is the Managing Director of Jacobs Investment Company LLC (JIC). JIC was created in 1997 to participate in real estate development throughout the United States. Additionally, Mr. Jacobs is a Board of Director of New Generation Technology (NGT) Nazareth, a technology incubator that supports and invests in innovation-based businesses and Israeli Arab entrepreneurs primarily in the life sciences and medical devices. Mr. Jacobs graduated in 1979 from the University of California at San Diego with a B.A. in Management Science.

Andy Nahas 3:30 PM Founder and President / The Prospect Fund

Andy is Founder and President of The Prospect Fund, a proprietary investment fund which seeks to maximize returns in the stock market in a socially responsible manner and also use its financial and analytical resources to support causes such as human rights, animal welfare, quality of life initiatives for the elderly, and student leadership efforts. The Prospect Fund is currently the #1 performing fund in its category (Stocks Only - no short selling) for the ten years ended December 2014, according to BarclayHedge.

Financial Horizons Conference

Held every year at UCSD’s Rady School of Management, the conference brings together hundreds of students, professionals, and alumni for an exciting day of networking and education. Students are invited to participate in six interactive workshops led by distinguished professionals from a myriad of financial industries including investment banking, private equity, venture capital, accounting and more.
